About Me

My name is Mohamed Naseem Mohamed. I passed the competitive examination in 1994 and received my first appointment at Talangaha Tamil School on February 5, 1996.

After completing my teacher training, I initially worked as a commerce teacher at Tundhuwa Muslim School. In 2003, I received a transfer to Uswatun Hasana Vidyalaya. However, with the education reforms of 2006, IT was introduced as a mainstream subject, and I transitioned to teaching Information and Communication Technology (ICT).

From a young age, I have had a strong desire to explore new developments in technology. Over the years, I have pursued various computer-related courses to deepen my knowledge.

I have served as a teacher for 28 years, including 18 years as an ICT teacher since 2006. During this time, I worked for 15 years at Uswatun Hasana Vidyalaya and 3 years at Muslim Ladies College. Additionally, I served as the teacher in charge of the computer labs at both schools.

I greatly value the Wisdom Cloud LMS program offered by the Provincial Ministry of Education, as it has significantly contributed to enhancing my knowledge and skills.

Today, I am very happy with my role as an ICT teacher. I see this as a wonderful opportunity to fulfill my long-held dream of working in this ever-evolving field.
